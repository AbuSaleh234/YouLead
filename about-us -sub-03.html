How we work

Our goal is to connect young leaders to learn, explore, demand, innovate, and create resilient sustainable solutions that aim at transforming our communities through:

Amplifying youth voices in key governance and development processes
Enhancing the capacity of young political leaders for them to meaningfully and effectively represent youth
Connecting and empowering young business leaders in their efforts to create economic opportunities

Our youth engagement model

We partner with state and nonstate Youth-led, Youth-serving organizations, and individuals to strategically position them for participation in leadership, governance and development processes.

We collaborate with national, regional, and international stakeholders to facilitate youth dialogues with policymakers and position youth to meaningfully participate in development processes.

We amplify youth voices at the national and regional level to provide local and global solutions.

We create youth-friendly platforms for youth to network and stay connected.

We create and connect youth to empowerment opportunities in politics and business

We enhance leadership capacity of youth to constructively participate in politics and effectively engage in business (Facilitate boot camps, summer academy and school, and training)

We advocate for improved youth-specific policy issues and participation in national and regional decision-making spaces

Who we work with

YouLead works with state and nonstate actors especially youth-led and youth service organizations, political leaders, business leaders, government leaders, development partners, regional, continental and multilateral institutions, CSO’s, artists, media and youth-led networks, think tanks, and thought leaders across Africa through equal recognition partnerships in the spirit of delivering as one.

Our key partners have included

  • African Union – relevant departments
  • Regional Economic Commissions (RECs)
  • National Associations of Young Parliamentarians
  • National Youth Councils (where they exist)
  • Multilateral Development Partners such as UN Agencies
  • Bilateral Development Partners such as Embassies
  • Aid Providers such as Official Development Assistance Agencies
  • International Non-Governmental Organisations
  • Regional and National Youth Umbrella Organisations
  • Civil Society Youth-led and Youth-serving organizations
  • Private Sector Apex Bodies – Business Communities
  • Youth-led businesses and young CEOs
