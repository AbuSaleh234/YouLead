Social Enterprise

Sale of Skills and Service

Our multi-talented team is available for tasks, proceeds of which directly support our work. As we look at our partnership arrangements, an area of opportunity for us to consider for partnership. This has quickly become one area through which we creatively raise funds for our work, through the sale of skills and services by the YouLead team as an alternative traditional fundraising means such as grants. Our partners, and those who have work-related to ours are now giving us tasks, which we perform, and instead of paying a consultant, they pay the agreed funds to the YouLead through the Training Centre for Development Cooperation.
Our "YouLead Faculty Team" of resource persons is composed of a pool of expert practitioners in training and capacity building, with expertise backgrounds in all key areas of youth engagement, mobilization and capacity enhancement to advance youth voice and participation.

Here below is a menu of services you can consider us for, as your option.
A. Consultancy and Technical Advisory Services - call us for anything youth-related
B. Issue-based youth-focused research - designing, execution and dissemination
C. BootCamps for Youth leaders and policymakers - planning and facilitation
D. Summer Academies and Schools on emerging issues specific to youth
E. Digital youth engagement - cross country internet-mediated work with youth
F. Program Ideation and Incubation - institutional and team mentorship
G. Strategic Communication and public engagement - positioning youth issues
H. Youth consultations and convenings through digital tools and virtual spaces

Sale of branded merchandise

Easy-buy branded items sold during events and marketed through our social media platforms. These include items such as Caps, T-shirts, and Mugs, to generate a small profit that can support in carrying out smaller activities in the project.
2. Grants & Donations
We accept grants and donations from social investors who are interested in supporting implementation of our program geared towards achieving YouLead Long Term ambitions. We keenly monitor our programs to ensure that the donor is satisfied with our deliverables.

3. Event Sponsorships
Companies, non-profits, and small businesses can give a certain amount of cash or incentives for purposes of hosting any event under this program. As a result, companies, nonprofits, and small businesses increased visibility and brand awareness of their work at the national and regional levels.
