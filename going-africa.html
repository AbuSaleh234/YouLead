GOING
AFRICA

Following an exciting 4-year journey (2017-2019) of building an innovative multistakeholder youth engagement model, in 2019, YouLead’s goal of inspiring PanAfrican Institutions such the African Union, Regional Economic Communities, United Nations and other Development Partner Agencies to observe and support the replication of YouLead’s youth engagement model. Since 2019, YouLead has received overwhelming requests for joint work with such institutions – bringing this goal to life. More, there is growing participation and demand for participation in YouLead’s work from youth across all the 54 African nations. With such PanAfrican partnerships and demand from African youth, it has become clear that YouLead has no choice but to respond to this call to become a PanAfrican youth engagement platform. This is our cast for our “Going Africa” since the turn of 2020.

To cover the entire continent with the AfCFTA Youth Inclusion Project, YouLead has incorporated a continental youth community of interest around the AfCFTA through a Continental ad hoc Youth Working Group and co-opted Collaborating Entities as support and mobilization structures. These elements and steps are directed toward increased continental ownership of AfCFTA among the leading community of youth-led initiatives especially those with a progressive voice on or interest in the AfCFTA already across the continent - the continental youth collective serves as a mass voice of African youth on matters of AfCFTA.

Jeunnesse Africaine contre La Corruption et pour la Transparence (J-ACT) for Central Africa, Southern Africa Youth Forum (SAYoF), and Southern Africa Youth Council (SADC Youth Council) for Southern Africa Youth Hub Africa for Western Africa, Atlas For Development for North Africa), and Nile Youth Development Actions (NYDA) for Horn of Africa are the Continental Collaborating Entities with whom we are implementing the AfCFTA Youth Inclusion Accelerator Project (AfCFTA-YIAP. As part of the AfCFTA-YIAP implementation, YouLead has just concluded a Continental Scoping Study on African Young Peoples’ Awareness of the AfCFTA and their perceived role as stakeholders in its implementation – proposed intervention areas under this partnership have been inspired by the study.
In this regard, YouLead works through partnerships with likeminded entities across the continental to reach more African youth. Our continental work is modeled along the African Regional Economic Communities (RECs) although activities and beneficiaries are not limited to REC members. In the spirit of United Nations Agenda 2030 – “Leaving no one Behind”, YouLead works with different stakeholders including organizations working with young people and these organizations are called YouLead Consortium members. YouLead Consortium members are youth centric organizations based in the EAC that support YouLead secretariat to promote YouLead agenda and work towards YouLead long term ambitions. They contribute in the planning, fundraising and execution of the YouLead projects, activities and events.

Together we have collaborated in:

  • Mobilizing and increasing the network of East African Young people
  • Organizing events and activities at country level
  • Support in delivering summits
  • Implementation of YouLead ambitions
  • Fundraising together with the secretariat
  • Technical expertise in different areas
  • Facilitate YouLead relationships with different governments and other stakeholders

    Eligibility Criteria

    Be a youth centric organization or youth led organization

    Be based or have activities in EAC (Burundi, Kenya, Rwanda, South Sudan, and Tanzania)

    Be a legally recognized organization

    Work on the same area(s) as YouLead:

    Youth in politics

    Youth in business, trade and enterprise

    Youth in peace and security

    Youth, gender equality and social inclusion

    Willingness to work together with YouLead and be involved in YouLead activities

    Willingness to involve YouLead in your organization’s activities

    Willingness to do fundraising together with YouLead

    Practice transparency and accountability

    Consistency and full commitment to agreed values, programs and activities

Membership

Membership of the YouLead Consortium shall be recognized by way of a signed Memorandum of Understanding valid for one year The Consortium membership will be subjected to an annual evaluation to assess the partnership.

Consortium members roles and responsibilities

Support in the implementation of YouLead activities and YouLead agenda
Collaborate with YouLead Secretariat in fundraising and resource mobilization for YouLead Summit and other joint programs.
Mobilize youth constituencies for joint programs and events
Roles and responsibilities of YouLead Secretariat
Facilitate access to national/regional/continental to policy making institutions
Offer regional/continental platform for engagement and influencing policy
Enhance visibility of consortium members across East Africa and the continent
Provide networking opportunities at regional and continental levels
Enhanced fundraising efforts through joint proposals
Extend technical assistance and support to members when needed
Expanded sharing opportunities such as youth capacity building

Membership Benefits

Members will have access to YouLead Action Network of alumni and partners for collaboration and engagement at regional level
One annual regional solidarity activity sponsored by YouLead and led by consortium members in line with YouLead areas of priorities
Fundraising as one; YouLead will support CM fundraising efforts by incorporating activities and budget line for them in all proposal submitted to national, regional, and international donors

Principles guiding our communication practice

Central coordination: Consortium members will work closely with the regional coordination team in the YouLead Secretariat to ensure that communication between Consortium members and YouLead Secretariat is consistent, timely and accurate
Acknowledgement of the partnership: Consortium members and YouLead Secretariat will both communicate on their website the existence of the partnership
Joint publicity: a Consortium members and YouLead will cross share content of mutual interests on their social media
Branding Authorisation: All public mention and use of YouLead Logos requires prior written approvals. Communication will be initiated by the lead organization in consultation with the regional coordinators and approved by YouLead communication
