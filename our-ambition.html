Our Ambition

We work towards a myriad of broad long-term ambitions, from which each annual theme focus is be crafted in consultation with the YouLead of and Partners and our Alumni Community – YouLead Action Network.

Our goal is to enhance solidarity, voice, participation, access to economic opportunities, and decent work for African youth hence contributing to a youth-centered development on the African continent

Long-term ambitions
Results Framework & Success Indicators

Ambition 1

Amplify Youth Voices in Key Regional Political Governance Institutions & Processes of the EAC and Accelerate Regional Integration

Success Indicator 1

Host the Annual YouLead Africa Summit

Success Indicator 2

Host the Annual Africa Under 40 Political Leaders Forum

Success Indicator 3

Host the Arusha Peace Forum- and annual East African Youth Forum on Sustainable Peace and Security

Success Indicator 4

A performance and cooperation peer review mechanism for elected young leaders to assess progress on their commitments & deepen their cooperation & partnerships

Success Indicator 5

Strengthen the capacity and deepen cooperation among Youth Councils where they exist and advocate for their establishment where they do not exist

Success Indicator 6

Build the capacity & increase the number of youth leaders competing for and holding positions of political responsibility in their countries at parliamentary & cabinet levels

Success Indicator 7

Host the annual Gender Equality Forum

Success Indicator 8

Establish an African Youth Leadership Mentorship and Fellowship Program to Nature and Nurture African Young Leaders
